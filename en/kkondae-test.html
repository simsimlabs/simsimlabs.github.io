In modern culture, the term "Boomer" (often referred to as "Kkondae" in Korea) has shifted from a generational label to a description of a specific mindset. It characterizes someone who is rigid in their ways, condescending to younger people, and insists that their past experiences are the only valid path to success.

SimSim Lab's Boomer Test is designed to analyze your professional and social communication style. Do you truly listen to new ideas, or do you find yourself starting sentences with "Back in my day"? Whether you are a Gen Zer acting like a veteran manager or a true Boomer with a youthful heart, this 15-question quiz will provide a fun and insightful look into your personality.

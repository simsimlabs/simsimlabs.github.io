Your chronological age counts the years since your birth, but your Mental Age determines how you perceive the world, handle emotions, and interact with society. Psychological maturity isn't always linear; some people remain youthful and inquisitive "children at heart" well into their 80s, while others display the wisdom and patience of an "old soul" during their teenage years.

SimSim Lab's Mental Age Test is an entertainment-based psychological tool that analyzes your daily habits, humor, stressors, and life values. Through 15 targeted questions, we map your mindset to a developmental stage ranging from a 5-year-old toddler to a 1000-year-old enlightened sage. Are you surprisingly mature or delightfully young?

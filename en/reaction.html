‚ö° Neural Reflexes and Cognitive Speed

The average human reaction time to a visual stimulus is approximately 250 milliseconds (0.25 seconds). This duration represents the time it takes for light to enter the eyes, be processed by the brain's visual cortex, and then trigger a motor command to the muscles. High-performance individuals, such as professional gamers, F1 racers, and sprinters, can often achieve reaction times below 180ms.

Factors such as age, fatigue, and even screen latency (input lag) can affect your performance on this test.
